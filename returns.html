Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery

Return Policy

Important: We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused, in original packaging with all tags and accessories intact.

Eligible for Return

  • Products with manufacturing defects or damages
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product not matching description
  • Missing parts or accessories
  • Changed your mind (within 7 days for non-electronics)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Products with signs of use or wear
  • Items without original packaging, tags, or accessories
  • Personalized or customized products
  • Perishable goods and intimate wear
  • Software products with opened seal
  • Products returned after 7 days from delivery

Return Timeline

Standard Products: 7 days from delivery date

Electronics: 3 days from delivery date

Books & Media: 7 days from delivery date

Fashion Items: 7 days from delivery date

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after item verification
  • Amount will be credited to your original payment method
  • For COD orders, refund will be processed to your bank account or Sansora wallet
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product is defective

Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Delivery

How can I track my order?
You can track your order by visiting the Track Order page and entering your order ID and email address. You'll also receive tracking updates via email and SMS once your order is shipped.
What are the delivery charges?
We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat delivery charge of ₹99 applies. Express delivery options are available at checkout for faster delivery.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive orders within 1-2 days. You can check the estimated delivery time for each product on its detail page.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused, in original packaging with tags intact. Electronics have a 3-day return window. Visit our Returns page to initiate a return.
How do I initiate a return?
Go to Orders > Select the item > Click "Return Item". Follow the instructions, pack the item securely, and our courier will pick it up from your address. You'll receive a confirmation email once the return is approved.
When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and verify the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method or Sansora wallet.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay), EMI options, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
Is it safe to use my credit card on Sansora?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and comply with PCI DSS standards. Your card details are never stored on our servers and all transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it's shipped. Go to Orders > Select order > Click "Cancel Order". If the order has already been shipped, you can refuse delivery or initiate a return once received.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?
Click on "Login" in the navigation bar, then select "Sign Up". Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password for your account.
How can I change my delivery address?
Go to Account Settings > Addresses > Add/Edit Address. You can save multiple addresses and select your preferred address during checkout. Make sure to update your address before placing an order.
